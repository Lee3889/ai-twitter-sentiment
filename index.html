AI Twitter Sentiment Dashboard

A One-Year Analysis of Public Reactions to Artificial Intelligence

Project Background

Artificial Intelligence became one of the most discussed topics on social media in 2023. This project analyzes daily AI-related tweet volume and sentiment scores on Twitter/X to explore how major AI events influenced public emotions and discussions.

Data Source

Dataset: Daily AI-related Twitter/X tweet counts and sentiment scores throughout 2023.

Methods: Line Chart, Sentiment Trend Analysis, Dual-Axis Visualization and Interactive Dashboard.

Interactive Dashboard

Key Events

  • GPT-4 Released — March 14, 2023
  • Llama 2 Released — July 18, 2023
  • OpenAI Leadership Crisis — November 17, 2023

Conclusion

The analysis reveals how major AI developments significantly influenced online discussions and public sentiment throughout 2023. Public attention and emotional responses often shifted alongside key technological announcements and industry events.
