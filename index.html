AI Twitter Sentiment Dashboard

A One-Year Analysis of Public Reactions to Artificial Intelligence

Project Background

Artificial Intelligence (AI) became one of the most influential and widely discussed technologies in 2023. Major developments such as the release of GPT-4, Llama 2, and other generative AI tools attracted unprecedented public attention and sparked extensive discussions across social media platforms.

This project focuses on AI-related conversations on Twitter/X and examines how public interest and sentiment changed throughout the year. By analyzing daily tweet volume and sentiment scores, we aim to identify patterns in public reactions and understand how major AI events influenced online discussions.

Through interactive visualizations and contextual event annotations, this project demonstrates the relationship between technological developments, public attention, and emotional responses in the AI era.

Data Source

Dataset: Daily AI-related Twitter/X tweet counts and sentiment scores throughout 2023.

Methods: Line Chart, Sentiment Trend Analysis, Dual-Axis Visualization and Interactive Dashboard.

Conclusion

Throughout 2023, public discussions about artificial intelligence experienced significant fluctuations in both volume and sentiment. Major technological announcements and industry events consistently influenced how people talked about and reacted to AI on social media.

The release of GPT-4 and Llama 2 attracted widespread attention and increased public optimism, while organizational uncertainty and industry controversies led to more complex emotional responses. These patterns demonstrate that social media sentiment is highly sensitive to major developments within the AI ecosystem.

By integrating tweet volume, sentiment analysis, and event annotations into an interactive dashboard, this project reveals the dynamic relationship between technological innovation and public perception. The findings highlight how data visualization can help transform large-scale social media data into meaningful insights and compelling stories.
