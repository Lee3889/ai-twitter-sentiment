AI Twitter Sentiment Dashboard

A One-Year Analysis of Public Reactions to Artificial Intelligence

Project Background

Artificial Intelligence became one of the most discussed topics on social media in 2023. This project analyzes daily AI-related tweet volume and sentiment scores on Twitter/X to explore how major AI events influenced public emotions and discussions.

Data Source

Dataset: Daily AI-related Twitter/X tweet counts and sentiment scores throughout 2023.
